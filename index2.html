Representing People with Diabetes in All Ten Regions of New York State, Regardless of Number or Type

CALL FOR ACTION

Help Fight For Guaranteed Healthcare

The New York Health Act (A.6058/S.5474 for the 2021-22 New York Legislative Session) is a single payer healthcare plan that ensures that nobody in the Great State of New York and any of it’s 10 vastly different Regions goes without healthcare. Approximately 1 million New York Residents are uninsured and millions more are underinsured. As people with diabetes we are constantly at risk of being underinsured or uninsured and having to pay thousands of dollars a month for vital life saving medications, the New York Health Act will prevent that. In addition, this bill will reduce the cost of the vital life saving medications that we take.
